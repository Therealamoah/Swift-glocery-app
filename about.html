About Swift Mart

Your trusted neighborhood grocery store now faster, smarter, and always fresh.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Swift Mart started with a simple idea: make grocery shopping fast, easy, and joyful for everyone.

We saw how busy life had become long queues, heavy bags, and wasted time. So we built an app that brings your favorite local store straight to your phone.

Today, we deliver fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat, and essentials to thousands of happy homes all in under 60 minutes.
